Who we are

Knowledge tech, located in Kabul, Afghanistan and other locations around the world is an independent, non-sectarian, modeled on the same company in UAE Network, System Engineering, Training, Procurement, Service, Management, and information systems. In the next few years, the company plans to enhance its existing services, add new features and programs in security, biometrics, IT Infrastructure, consulting and computer engineering. The quality of services provided by KNOWLEDGE is based on its highly qualified senior staff and mid-level technicians who hold advanced degrees from leading universities and certifications from well-known vendors such as Cisco, Microsoft, Juniper, IBM, Dell etc.

About 90 projects from throughout Afghanistan are now completed by Knowledge solutions Afghanistan. Growth is expected to expand by 2015. These projects are from US donor-funded projects, US Army, Military services, Government and Afghan-owned companies.
In addition to these, KNOWLEDGE has had leading projects in development of the country that would have bigger impact on how people live on projects such as fiber connectivity, WiMAX expansion, internetworking between offices with LAN speed, Training of governmental employees, security breach testing for sensitive data, cloud storage and many other activities that are not only being productive to an office/company but has impact on the way the country works. Over the course of time that KNOWLEDGE has been busy in Afghanistan, we are now borderless in the country with footprints in every part of Afghanistan.
The Company is not a short-term project, but a permanent company for the people of Afghanistan. Its impact will be felt with the growth of the company itself. Its projects—both private and non-private— will help Afghanistan’s become a better standard in information systems. In business and government. They will bring to those roles the values to which Knowledge is committed: free expression, equality of opportunity, ethical behavior, mutual respect, academic rigor, service to others, and hard work which will result in your companies better and the smooth transition of becoming an economic leader from a war-torn country.
